FAIRNESS & BIAS IN ML

Kvistholt Photography

With Machine Learning algorithms in use in so many industries to optimize decision making, we need to ensure that these decisions not only scale profit for business but act objectively.

That task is easier said than done because fairness is a principle hardly actualized in our current society because of the wide range of perceptions of fairness. Everyone has their own perspective on the issue.

In the field of fairness we're asking the questions:

  • How do we define fairness universally?
  • How do we implement fairness measures?
  • How do we evaulate model/ algorithmic fairness?
  • What policies guide the implementation & evaluation process?
  • How do protect everyone's best interests in the process?
